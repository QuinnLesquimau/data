If $X$ is $T_3$, given an open $U \subset X$ and $x \in U$ there is an open $V$ with $x \in V \subset cl(V) \subset U$. Thus the set of all such $int(cl(V))$ forms a basis for $X$.

See page 17 of {{doi:10.1007/978-1-4612-6290-9}}.
