If $X$ is $T_1$, any nontrivial connected subset is dense-in-itself. Thus if $X$ is scattered and $T_1$, it has no nontrivial connected subsets.

Asserted on Figure 9 of {{doi:10.1007/978-1-4612-6290-9}}.
