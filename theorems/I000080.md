If $X$ is Urysohn and $x,y \in X$ are distinct, there is an $f:X \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$ with $f(x) \neq f(y)$.

Asserted on page 223 of {{doi:10.1007/978-1-4612-6290-9}}.
