For any $A \subseteq X$ we have that $$\overline{A} = \begin{cases}
A, &\text{if $A$ is finite} \\\
A \cup \lbrace p \rbrace, &\text{if $A$ is infinite.}
\end{cases}$$
If $A$ is countable, then so is $\overline{A}$, so $A$ cannot be dense.
