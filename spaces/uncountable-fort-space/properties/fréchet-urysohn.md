Let $A\subseteq X$ and let $a\in\overline A$. If $a\neq p$ then $a$ is isolated and hence $a\in A$. Let us assume that $a=p$. Then pick any injective sequence in $X\setminus\{p\}$. This sequence converges to $p$.
