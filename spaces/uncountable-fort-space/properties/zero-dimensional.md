$$\mathcal{B} = \lbrace \lbrace x \rbrace : x \in X \setminus \lbrace p \rbrace \rbrace \cup \lbrace X \setminus F : F \subseteq X \setminus \lbrace p \rbrace\text{ is finite} \rbrace$$
is a base consisting of clopen sets.
