Given $p < q \in \mathbb{Q}$, let $\alpha$ be irrational with $p < \alpha < q$. Then $( - \infty , \alpha ) \cap \mathbb{Q}$, $(\alpha , + \infty ) \cap \mathbb{Q}$ are disjoint open neighborhoods of $p,q$, respectively whose union is $\mathbb{Q}$.
